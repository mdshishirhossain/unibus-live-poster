01 · About

Introduction

UniBus Live's passenger app is built on React Native with Expo SDK 56 — one JavaScript codebase that runs on Android today and is iOS-ready with no rewrite. The result is a polished, real-time, bilingual app that puts the bus in every student's pocket.

02 · Goals

Objectives

  • Open the app → see your bus on a live map in under 3 seconds
  • Pre-trip view: know the bus is on its way before it departs
  • Push alerts when bus is near your subscribed stop
  • Plan ahead: see today's, tomorrow's, and all-week schedules
  • Full English + বাংলা (Bengali) toggle on every screen
Problem

Wait time you can't predict.

  • Students wait blindly — no idea when the bus will arrive
  • Existing transport WhatsApp groups deliver stale, fragmented info
  • No way to plan classes, breaks, or commutes around bus timing
  • Language barrier — most transit apps are English-only

04 · Principles

Design Principles

Five decisions shaped every screen — chosen before code was written and held to throughout the build.

  • Real-time first. No manual reloads — Socket.IO + polling fallback.
  • Bilingual from day one. Every string typed and translated.
  • Native-feel performance. Hermes AOT compile · ProGuard · arm64-only APK.
  • Battery-aware GPS. Driver app uses foreground service; passenger uses balanced mode.
  • Accessible by default. WCAG-style hit targets · Reduce Motion · screen reader labels.
