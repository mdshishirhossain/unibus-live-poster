01 · About

Introduction

UniBus Live answers a simple question every university student asks every morning: "Where's my bus?" The platform fuses driver-phone GPS, hardware GPS devices, and a real-time backend into one polished app — so passengers see live positions on a map, get push alerts before their stop, and admins manage the whole fleet from one dashboard.

02 · Goals

Objectives

  • Show every active bus on a live, accurate map
  • Cut wait time at stops with ETA + stop alerts
  • Pre-trip visibility — see the bus before it departs
  • Empower admins with full fleet & routes control
  • Inclusivity via English + বাংলা (Bengali)
Problem Statement

Riders wait blindly. Admins fly blind.

  • Students wait at stops with no clue when the bus will come
  • Existing WhatsApp groups give stale, fragmented information
  • Drivers don't communicate delays or route deviations
  • No central dashboard for fleet, trips, or live operations
  • Language barrier — most transit tools are English-only

06 · Process

Methodology

1
GPS source emits location
Driver phone or bus hardware sends lat/lng every 1–5 sec.
2
Backend ingests & normalises
Smoothing, stale detection, idempotent storage, audit log.
3
Pre-trip phase derived
AT_DEPOT → APPROACHING_ORIGIN → AT_ORIGIN via geofence + speed.
4
Auto-start on origin dwell
30s within origin + sustained movement → trip RUNNING.
5
Realtime fan-out
Socket.IO push to all subscribers + REST polling fallback.
6
Passenger sees ETA + alerts
Live map, stop progression, push notification before arrival.
